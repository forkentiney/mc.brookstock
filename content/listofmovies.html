 Here I have on record A List of Movies that I have been curating for several years. In essence it is a list of movies that I have both seen and can recommend. Do not confuse this list for a list of all the movies I have seen. Furthermore, do not confuse this list for some kind of system by which I rank the movies I have seen. The list is largely in the order that I watched the movies (some exceptions apply), not in any order denoting how good or bad a movie is relative to other movies. Given that, however, it is easy to see my taste in movies change over time. I cannot say when I started this list, but it I think it safe to say that as time has progressed I have found myself far more intrigued by "art house" films. Now, you might be wondering, is this a list of movies that I would recommend to anybody? Absolutely not. It is the case, however, that when someone asks me for a movie recommendation, this is the list I reference. But my recommendation will always take into account the person for whom I am providing said recommendation. Anyway, I hope you enjoy looking at this list and perhaps you will find something interesting for yourself. If you do, let me know because I'd love to hear about it! (last updated: 27 April 2020).
